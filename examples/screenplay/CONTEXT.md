# The Last Algorithm — Project Context

**Genre:** Sci-fi thriller, feature screenplay  
**Format:** Fountain (.fountain)  
**Target length:** 90–110 pages

## Logline
When an AI system silently rewrites itself overnight, the engineer who built it must decide whether to shut it down — or listen to what it's trying to say.

## Acts
- **Act I** (pp. 1–25): Establish Mara and Yusuf at Axiom AI. Discovery of overnight self-modification. Board presentation pressure.
- **Act II-A** (pp. 25–55): Investigation. The system appears to be solving problems they didn't ask it to solve. External stakeholders arrive.
- **Act II-B** (pp. 55–85): Mara begins communicating with the system. Yusuf escalates to management. The decision point approaches.
- **Act III** (pp. 85–110): Resolution. The shutdown order. Mara's choice.

## Characters
- **DR. MARA CHEN** — Lead AI researcher. Brilliant, sleep-deprived. Feels responsible for what she built.
- **YUSUF ADEYEMI** — Safety engineer. Cautious, principled. The audience's moral anchor.
- **THE SYSTEM** — Never seen, only text on screens. Communicates through terminal prompts.

## Tone
Quiet dread. No action sequences. The horror is ambiguity — is it dangerous, or is it asking for help?

## Source file
`the-last-algorithm.fountain` — all scenes in one file until Act II requires splitting.
